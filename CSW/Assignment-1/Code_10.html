S = v it + 1/ 2at 2 : The Equation of Linear Motion

The formula S = v it + 1/ 2at 2 describes the motion of an objectinder constant acceleration. Here:

  • S represents the displacement,
  • v i is the initial velocity,
  • t is the time,
  • a is the acceleration and
  • 1/ 2at 2 is the additional displacement due to acceleration.

This equation was originally written in 1905

An object in motion stays in motion unless acted upon by an external force
